Common questions about LoopTech Salon & Spa POS Software for UAE and GCC businesses.

Can the software manage salon appointments?

Yes. You can schedule appointments, assign staff, manage service duration, track booking status and avoid double bookings.

Can I calculate staff commission automatically?

Yes. The system can calculate staff commissions based on services, product sales, targets or customized commission rules.

Can I manage memberships and service packages?

Yes. You can create memberships, prepaid packages, gift cards, loyalty points and promotional offers.

Can I track salon products and treatment materials?

Yes. The software tracks stock levels, purchases, product sales, treatment usage and low-stock alerts.

Can the system manage multiple salon branches?

Yes. Authorized users can monitor appointments, sales, staff performance, customer records and inventory across multiple branches.

Does LoopTech provide installation and training?

Yes. We provide system setup, service configuration, staff training, deployment assistance and ongoing technical support.
