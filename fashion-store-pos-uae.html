FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Fashion Store POS Software Questions

Learn more about LoopTech Fashion and Garment Store POS Software for boutiques, clothing stores and multi-branch retailers.

Can the software manage multiple sizes and colours?

Yes. Each fashion product can have multiple sizes, colours, styles, brands, patterns and collections. The available stock quantity of every variant can be tracked separately.

Does it support barcode scanners and receipt printers?

Yes. LoopTech Fashion POS can work with compatible barcode scanners, barcode label printers, receipt printers and cash drawers.

Can I process fashion product returns and exchanges?

Yes. The system supports product returns, size and colour exchanges, refunds and store-credit transactions while maintaining accurate invoice and inventory records.

Can I manage seasonal offers and loyalty programs?

Yes. You can create seasonal promotions, fixed or percentage discounts, coupons, loyalty points and customer-specific offers.

Can I manage multiple fashion store branches?

Yes. You can monitor sales, inventory, employees, cashiers and stock transfers for multiple branches from one central dashboard.

Is the Fashion POS available as cloud or hybrid software?

Yes. LoopTech can provide cloud, online, on-premise or hybrid deployment depending on your internet connection and business requirements.

Does LoopTech provide installation and staff training?

Yes. We provide software installation, system configuration, product data setup, staff training, data migration and ongoing technical assistance.

Is this software suitable for small boutiques?

Yes. The software can be configured for small boutiques, single fashion stores, garment shops, textile showrooms and large multi-branch retail businesses.
